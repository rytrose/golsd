Experimentation

The Spring Grove Experiment was a series of studies on the use of LSD to treat alcoholism. There is a fascinating 1965 documentary that dives into the experiment, and I was drawn in by the people in the film.

This is an experiment of my own, exploring the musicality of voice that has always intrigued me. It is also a technical experiment, which you can read about here.

The vocal samples were curated by me and are played back at random, with associated rhythmic patterns created on a Yamaha DD-9 from my childhood.
